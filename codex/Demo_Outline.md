# 30-Minute Demo Outline

## 0:00–5:00 — Arrival & Tutorial Crisis
- **Scenario:** Mid-town bridge collapse with stalled traffic.
- **Player choices:**
  - Stabilize supports (slow, safe) vs. evacuate civilians (fast, risky).
  - Use heat vision to weld beams vs. super strength to brace.
- **Outcomes:** City health adjusts; tutorial introduces CCS and HCE.

## 5:00–12:00 — Villain Interference
- **Scenario:** Villain broadcasts a manifesto and stages a distraction.
- **Player choices:**
  - Investigate the broadcast source vs. rescue ongoing incidents.
  - Confront villain directly vs. stop collateral damage first.
- **Outcomes:** VPE escalates tactics; narrative tone shifts.

## 12:00–20:00 — Multi-Point Emergency
- **Scenario:** Simultaneous fires and falling debris across two districts.
- **Player choices:**
  - Prioritize high-risk civilians vs. protect key infrastructure.
  - Use flight speed to triage vs. careful precision.
- **Outcomes:** District ratings diverge; population trust changes.

## 20:00–26:00 — Villain Set Piece
- **Scenario:** A super-powered device threatens a power grid.
- **Player choices:**
  - Disable the device with heat vision (fast, risky) vs. contain with strength.
  - Negotiate with villain (dialogue check) vs. forceful takedown.
- **Outcomes:** Branching mission tags lock in for finale.

## 26:00–30:00 — Resolution & Debrief
- **Scenario:** Aftermath reports and city status recap.
- **Player choices:**
  - Address press questions vs. leave silently.
  - Plan next response (setup for future demo slice).
- **Outcomes:** Telemetry summary prompts and player feedback capture.
